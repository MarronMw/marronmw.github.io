Product and Services

Air Flight Ticketing

We offer both domestic and international ticketing. In order to provide effective and efficient service to our valued clients, we work with two computer reservations systems. We also help our clients to get preferable seats and other special requests on any flight of your choice. Our team is dedicated and will ensure serve travel options to our clients for comparison purposes to ensure that our clients get value for their money.

Visa Processing

We also offer visa processing services at the request of our esteemed clients for China and Dubai only when served with client’s coloured passport sized photo, copy of passport and a valid itinerary.

Travel Insurance

We facilitate insurance for our client’s trips to ensure the clients are covered in case of loss and damage to their property.

Car Hire Bookings / Airport Transfers

We have good working relationship with reputable car hire companies for best cars and airport shuttles ready for all our clients on request. We also negotiated favorable rates for car hire rentals for the best domestic hires.

Accommodation

To make bookings simpler and more cost effective we consolidate accommodation inventory to serve the clients better and effectively. Our team is specialized and dedicated in picking best accommodation at affordable rates for all destinations.
